Trading Bot Crypto FAQ Help Center

Frequently asked questions regarding trading fees, liquidity, slippage, and safety.

Do I need to deposit funds into the DEX?

No. Unlike centralized exchanges, Trading Bot Crypto is non-custodial. You trade directly from your own wallet, meaning you retain control over your assets at all times.

What is slippage?

Slippage is the price shift that occurs when a trade changes the pool's asset ratio. Large trades relative to pool depth experience higher slippage. You can inspect market depths on the CoinGecko market caps directory.

How do I earn fees on the DEX?

You can earn fees by depositing equal values of token pairs into the liquidity pools. The contract pays you a proportional share of the exchange fees generated by trades, powering decentralized finance (DeFi).
