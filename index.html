How it works

Trading Bot Crypto setup in three real steps

Use the app to shape a bot workflow around DeFi markets, live route data and wallet-controlled execution. The page does not invent returns or promise outcomes - the app shows the current inputs before any action.

1

Choose the market

Select the asset, chain and trading direction you want to automate. ETH, BTC-style exposure and supported network options are handled inside the app.

2

Set the rules

Define the entry logic, execution limits and wallet permissions. The app keeps the bot configuration explicit so you can review each assumption.

3

Review and launch

Check the live quote, route, cost details and safety prompts in the dashboard, then decide whether to connect your wallet and approve the action.

Why use it

Bot automation without giving up wallet control

FAQ

Trading Bot Crypto questions

01

What is Trading Bot Crypto?

Trading Bot Crypto is a non-custodial DeFi app for setting up crypto trading bot workflows, previewing route details and reviewing execution settings before using the dashboard.

02

Does a crypto trading bot guarantee profit?

No. Crypto markets move quickly, and automation can amplify both good and bad decisions. Use bot logic as a tool, not as a promise of returns.

03

Which assets and chains are supported?

The app is built around major crypto assets such as Bitcoin exposure and Ethereum-based routes. See the full network list on the supported chains page.

04

How long does setup take?

Setup time depends on your strategy, wallet, selected chain and current network conditions. The app shows the required inputs and live quote details before you continue.

05

What does it cost?

Network costs, route pricing and any app limits are shown inside the dashboard before you approve anything. No fixed fee or result is claimed on this page.

06

Is the bot custodial?

No. The app is framed for wallet-controlled DeFi use. You review permissions, connect your own wallet and decide what to approve.
